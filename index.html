Welcome to PeakPoint Fashion House

Background

PeakPoint Fashion House is a start-up retail clothing business established in Maseru, Lesotho. The business operates as a physical fashion store specialising in stylish, high-quality women’s wear. The name “PeakPoint” symbolises excellence and reaching the highest level of style, while “Fashion House” reflects professionalism, creativity, and a strong brand identity within the fashion industry. The business operates under the slogan, “Where Confidence Meets Couture,” which represents its commitment to empowering women through elegant and fashionable clothing.
